## Summary vs DreamForge Estimates

| Service | DreamForge | Market Floor | Market Mid | Position |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Shopify setup | $800-1.5k | $500 | $2-5k | Below mid |
| Website | $1.5-3k | $2k | $3-8k | Below mid |
| Automation | $2.5-6k | $500 | $1.5-5k | At/above mid |
| Custom SaaS | $4-12k | $5k | $10-30k | Well below mid |
| IT retainer | $250-1k/mo | $500/mo | $1-2k/mo | Below floor |
| Training | $1-2.5k | $2k | $2-5k | Below mid |

**Conclusion:** DreamForge is positioned as affordable/accessible across most tiers. Automation pricing is competitive. Shopify, SaaS, and IT support are notably below market — intentional strategy to win on value.
