# Phase 1 Market Research — 2026-03-20

Southwest/Central Florida small business sectors underserved by technology.

## Ranked Opportunity Table

| # | Sector | Pain Points | DreamForge Fit | Est. Budget | Competition |
|---|--------|-------------|----------------|-------------|-------------|
| 1 | **Independent Home Health Agencies** | EVV compliance (legally mandated), paper scheduling, no family portal, credential tracking | Custom scheduling + compliance + family portal | $5–12k + retainer | Low for small agencies |
| 2 | **Paver / Enclosure / Aluminum Contractors** | No CRM, paper estimates, no job tracker, change order disputes | Estimate-to-payment portal + e-signature + client comms | $4–10k + retainer | Very Low — niche completely untouched by tech |
| 3 | **Pool & Spa Service Companies** | Paper route sheets, chemical logs, 30-day invoice lag, no client portal | Route automation + recurring billing + client portal | $3–9k + retainer | Moderate (tools exist, adoption very low) |
| 4 | **Estate Liquidation / Consignment** ⭐ | Manual inventory, consignor payout disputes, no online preview/presale | Consignor portal + inventory + automated payouts | $3.5–8k + retainer | Very Low — only 2-3 weak competitors nationally |
| 5 | **Independent STR Co-Hosts** (5–40 properties) ⭐ | Text-based owner comms, manual payouts, no maintenance ticketing | Owner portal + turnover automation + reporting | $4–10k + retainer | Low for boutique segment |
| 6 | **Mobile Wellness Practitioners** ⭐ | No mobile-aware booking, paper intake/SOAP notes, high no-show rate | Booking + client records + route scheduling | $2.5–7k + retainer | Low — existing tools not built for mobile/in-home |

⭐ = unexpected/niche — not on standard "Florida small business tech" lists

## Strategic Notes

- **Best immediate targets:** Paver/contractors (post-hurricane cash, no tech), Estate liquidation (Florida-dense, near-zero competition), Home health (EVV compliance creates urgency)
- **Estate liquidation** is uniquely strong in SW Florida due to aging population generating steady estate sales
- **STR co-hosts** are booming in Sarasota, Siesta Key, Cape Coral, Anna Maria Island — boutique segment (5–40 properties) is underserved by enterprise tools
- **Selling approach:** Relationship-driven for contractors and estate/STR operators — not SaaS sign-up flows

- Sector 5: Independent STR Co-Hosts (5–40 properties)
