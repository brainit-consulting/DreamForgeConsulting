# Phase 1: Market Research — March 19, 2026

## Sectors Researched

| # | Sector | Pain Points | DreamForge Service Fit | Typical Budget | Competition |
|---|--------|-------------|----------------------|----------------|-------------|
| 1 | Property Management (small landlords, 5-50 units) | Manual tenant tracking, paper leases, spreadsheet rent collection, no maintenance portal | Client portal + automated billing + maintenance ticketing | $4-10k | Medium — big players (AppFolio, Buildium) too expensive/complex for small operators |
| 2 | Home Services (pool, pest, lawn, cleaning) | Paper scheduling, phone-only booking, manual routing, no invoicing system | Booking automation + route optimization + invoicing portal | $2.5-5k | High — GorillaDesk, Housecall Pro exist, but many still don't use them |
| 3 | Senior / Home Health Services | Paper care plans, manual scheduling, no family communication portal, compliance tracking gaps | Client/family portal + scheduling + AHCA compliance dashboard | $5-10k | Low — most solutions target large agencies, not 5-20 employee shops |
| 4 | **Marine Services (boat repair, detailing, storage)** | Phone-only booking, no online presence, paper work orders, manual invoicing | Website + booking system + work order tracking | $2.5-6k | Very Low — almost no SaaS targets this niche in FL |
| 5 | **Independent Insurance Agencies** | Disconnected quoting tools, manual client follow-ups, paper policy tracking, no CRM | CRM + automated follow-ups + client portal | $4-8k | Medium — Salesforce is overkill, most use spreadsheets |

## Selected Sectors
- **Marine Services** (#4)
- **Independent Insurance Agencies** (#5)
